payment plans for my commissions

I try to make is as easy as possable to pay for your suit!

Option 1

you will make 2 payments

  • 1 down payment (materals + 50% of the labor costs)
  • 1 payment before I ship (50% labor costs + shiping fees)

Plan option 2

you will make 4 payments

  • 1 down payment (materals + 25% of the labor costs)
  • 2 payments as I work on the suit (25% of the labor costs each)
  • 1 payment before I ship (25% labor costs + shiping fees)

Plan option 3

you will make 8 payments

  • 1 down payment (materals + 25% of the labor costs)
  • 6 payments as I work on the suit (8% of the labor costs each)
  • 1 payment before I ship (25% labor costs + shiping fees)

Plan option 4

you will pay full price for the down payment

  • 1 down payment (materals + 100% of the labor costs + shiping fees)
