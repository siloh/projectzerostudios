Fursuit care and infromation!

My fursuit making process!

  1. I gather the materials
  2. I make the foam base
  3. I make other facial features, like eyes, nose, and toung!
  4. I make the base for the ears and set them aside
  5. I duct tape the base and get the pattern
  6. I cut out the pattern in my fur and shave parts as needed
  7. I pin the fur to the base to make sure evry thing looks good
  8. I sew all the fur peices togther
  9. I glue the fur down
  10. i make the pattern for the ears
  11. I sew and glue the ear fur on
  12. I glue and sew the ears to the head
  13. I add fleece and teeth to the inside of the mouth
  14. I add fabirc around the eyes
  15. I make sure everythings sewn properly and looks good

full washing

  • fill a tub with slightly warm water (not too hot!)
  • fill with soap or fur safe laundrey detergent!
  • soak the suit item in the water, suporting the ears
  • take it out and brush the fur the way you want it to go
  • let dry, putting suport on the ears

dissenfecting

  • fill a spray bottle with 50% rubbing alcohol and 50% essental oil ( for scent)
  • stuff paper towels in the eyes to protect the paint!
  • spray the entire suit with a light mist
  • spray the insides and place on a fan to dry
